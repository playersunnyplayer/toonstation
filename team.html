Nic Camecho (Mkbs) is approaching his 40’th year in the Film and TV Industry.

After graduating the first NFTS intensive Film course in Cinematography (1981) his first job was in News and 2nd Unit Camera. After branching into VFX Motion Control and working up to Supervisor in 1989 Nic, ran Europe’s first Live Action Motion Control Camera on a host of marquee commercials.

Nic’s creative side grew in parallel from Storyboard Artist, Animator, Model Lighting and VFX Design in both 2D Animation and Film VFX until culminating as Animation Supervisor with Disney TV in 1996. In 2002 Nic further branched into CG as Layout / Camera, Animator, Modeler, Lighting and Comp and finally to Creative Director overall on a host of award winning TV, Games and Movie projects.

In 2011 he started the PreViz Dept at Prime Focus for a ‘Lego’ TV series spanning Mumbai, London and Bangkok. In 2014 Nic again teamed up with his Mo Con Team at The VFX Co, working with NVizage, The Third Floor, Proof Inc, Framestore and MPC on PreVIz, TechViz and taking on the R n D and On Set Supervision for NCAM with real time camera tracking in VP, AR and VR several marquee Movies through to the end of 2016.

For the past 5 years Nic has worked as Creative Supervisor and VFX Producer across several CG Film and Streamed TV series projects and is currently working as D.o.P. and PreViz Director on a UK based CG Movie.
